Shang Chi

Author: Stan Lee

Main character: Shang Chi

Shang Chi begins his training in the martial arts. He is sent to assasinate the leader of the Iron Gang.

After fulfiling his mission, a a traumatized Shang Chi escapes to San Franciso, where he adopts the name "Shaun". Everything was going well until some one from his past turns up unexepectedly.
